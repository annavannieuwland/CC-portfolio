Consentful Interface

Ideal Consentful Flowchart

Design & Context

The goal was to search for clear and creative ways to disclose information to my user, and aim to communicate consent.

This should cover the following four categories from FRIES: Freely given, Informed, Enthusiastic, Specific.

The opening screen informs, is specific, and contains a yes button to ensure someone is freely giving consent. The feeling of enthusiasm is harder to cover, but was tried to be translated by an enthusiastic button.

Design Process

There was supposed to be an extra check, similarly to the flowchart to ensure enthusiasm. Sadly, I had troubles linking different scenes, so the consent was only one time.
