LSD Spongebob

Description

The goal of this second week was to generate a face, which could be randomised through an instructions of selected variables. We had to implement the following technical components in our sketch:

*User-defined variables (),

*random (),

*mousePressed() or keyPressed().

The image changes shape and colour upon clicking your mouse, and some of the ‘holes’ response to the location of the mouse on the screen.

Design Process

I was a bit intimidated by online sketching after our first project. I felt like I had little control online and could not translate my ideas into code. Therefor I started by physically drawing the face I wanted to create.

I wanted to implement everything I was shown in the class and lecture into my drawing, to make esure I propperly understood them and felt comfortable using the new skills. That is why I tried to lpay with random colour, size, and placement as possible.

Reflection

I translated a different sketch to p5js, since I realised my drawing was too ambitious for my skillset. I am happy I simplified the project, as I had a lot of fun during the programming process! Everything worked out as I wanted, and I drew with a lot more understanding compared to the first project. There was a lot less trial and error and more intend during this project.
